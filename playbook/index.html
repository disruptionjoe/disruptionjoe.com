Why It Works

It is facilitation first.

Most AI workshops assume the room is already ready. Joe's playbook assumes the room is mixed: some people are curious, some skeptical, some ahead, some behind. The structure is built for that reality.

The point is not to impress a team with AI. The point is to get a team working together well enough that the right AI moves become obvious.

That is why the system combines hands-on experimentation, room-reading, prioritization, and design-thinking pressure instead of treating the session like a lecture. Good facilitation makes better AI work possible.
