Discovery Call

Make better decisions, faster.

If your team needs help getting practical with AI, this is a good place to start. But I find that teams often have deeper issues. I've spent the last decade as an operator responsible for getting teams unstuck. My value has been in bringing clarity and momentum when work is bogging down somewhere between strategy and execution.

Rather than another org chart redesign, these teams need clarity on why effort isn't turning into results. That might be because of low AI adoption, but usually it is some mix of unclear priorities, cross-functional friction, weak follow-through, or some mix of the above. This conversation helps sort that out and figure out whether a team AI session, a focused workshop, or deeper support would be the most useful next step.

  • Best when the team has talent and good intent, but momentum is missing.
  • Good fit when the pain is obvious, but the right next move is not.
  • Team AI sessions can save weeks of research distracting from priorities.
