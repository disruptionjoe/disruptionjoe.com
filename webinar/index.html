Why so many AI pilots stall out

An interactive 60-minute session for team leaders, operators, founders, and managers who want to see and feel how the right methods turn curious or skeptical team members into drivers of AI adoption leading to lasting change.

Most AI conversations stay too abstract for too long. Teams talk about tooling, training, adoption, and resistance, but they still cannot see where the real friction is: where work gets unclear, where signal gets lost, and where people need different kinds of support.

This is a live proof experience. Instead of listening to a lecture about AI transformation, you will watch a room use AI, compare interpretations, and turn scattered input into something more usable.

You will leave with
  • A clearer picture of the kinds of friction teams run into when AI enters real workflows
  • A concrete example of how Joe facilitates a room from vague signal to shared insight
  • A better sense of what kind of next step would actually help: discovery call, team diagnostic, or a lighter follow-up
Who this is for
  • Team leaders trying to understand where AI is creating confusion, drag, or uneven adoption
  • Operators and managers who need a practical way to surface workflow friction
  • Founders or executive sponsors evaluating whether outside facilitation would help a team move faster
  • Individuals who want to experience the method, even if the design center is team work
What to expect
  • Not a passive webinar: you will use an LLM during the session
  • Built for mixed starting points; no advanced prompting background required
  • Short breakouts or full-room analysis depending on room size
  • A practical decode at the end showing what happened and why it matters
