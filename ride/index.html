The Activations Playbook

How a session moves.

01

Arcs

The shape of a session.
An arc is the journey we move through together. From where you are now to a place where the next move is obvious.
02

Plays

Structured exercises.
A play is a small repeatable activity inside an arc. Tested in real sessions, not improvised on the day.
03

Moves

Reusable AI interactions.
A move is a specific way of working with the model. Once you've felt it, you can use it on your own afterward.
04

Outputs

Artifacts you keep.
Every play produces something tangible. Notes, frameworks, named decisions. You leave with the work itself.
